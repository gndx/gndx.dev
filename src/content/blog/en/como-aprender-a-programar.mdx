## Do you want to start programming?

Many times taking the first step can be difficult and complicated, that is why I propose a series of tips so that you can start programming. The first thing you should keep in mind is that programming is a whole science, a series of methodologies and understandings about the world of computing.

At first it can be overwhelming, especially because you will find a lot of information on the internet, resources, books, courses, videos, among many other information, but with clear objectives, simple planning where you dedicate time and practice, you will be able to understand the programming and achieve your objectives whether by changing careers or starting a new one.
