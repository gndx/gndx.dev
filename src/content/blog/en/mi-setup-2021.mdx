#### Starting...

Today marks one year since I started in the world of streaming and content creation on YouTube/Twitch. Throughout all this time I have invested in having the best quality in terms of content creation, which is why I have invested a lot in acquiring each of the pieces that make up my setup to continue creating content with higher quality.

I leave you a brief summary of some important events and the evolution of the final setup.

#### March 2020

My first YouTube broadcast, without expecting anything, happened and I actually ended up liking it a lot.
As you can see, I had almost no equipment to transmit, a camera and a simple microphone, those were my beginnings.

![enter image description here](https://arepa.s3.amazonaws.com/gndx-mi-setup-2020.jpg)

#### June 2020

We managed to obtain the Partner on Twitch which allowed us to verify the account and create the Team for the community of creators in Spanish #EStreamerCoders.

![enter image description here](https://arepa.s3.amazonaws.com/gndx-twitch-partner.jpg)

#### August 2020

By this time my wife @[anabelisam\_](https://twitter.com/anabelisam_) started streaming, creating management and backend content.

![enter image description here](https://arepa.s3.amazonaws.com/gndx-setup-ana-belisa.jpg)

#### September 2020

With a more organized space, with accessories to create content, it already had better quality in light, audio and video. I had the opportunity to be one of the streamers at Platzi CONF 2020, putting into practice my knowledge and tools that I had created for streamers. Here the Bebeloper was already part of the Staff.

![enter image description here](https://arepa.s3.amazonaws.com/gndx-mi-setup-bebeloper.jpg)

#### November 2020

Now I had a program in Platzi, where I interviewed professors and professionals in the software development industry, this time I had to be the host and streamer for the Microsoft community in Platzi.

![enter image description here](https://arepa.s3.amazonaws.com/gndx-setup-microsoft.jpg)

#### January 2021

After a break for vacation and a move, opening a new workspace, a room for the studio dedicated to content creation.

![enter image description here](https://arepa.s3.amazonaws.com/gndx-setup-new-home.jpg)

#### March 2021

We have finally completed version 1.0.0 of the studio in the new house located in Sopó Cundinamarca, connecting nature and technology.

![enter image description here](https://arepa.s3.amazonaws.com/gndx-setup-2021.jpg)
