## Why do you need a website?

Think about it: What is the first thing someone does when you talk to them about your project, business or whatever? Exactly, it googles you!
A website is your letter of introduction on the Internet. It is the space where you decide what to show and how to do it.
It doesn't matter if you sell empanadas, are an illustrator, psychologist, streamer or just want to put your portfolio online.
Having your own domain (your name or that of your business) is no longer a luxury, it is basic and necessary.

## What the heck is a .com domain and what is it for?

It's simple, the domain is your Internet address that is **easy** to access and that is memorable among people.

Do you want to be found easily? You need something like **tunegocio.com**.
A good domain is like a catchy nickname, easy to remember, nothing like “negociosuperpro789-especial.com” (don't complicate things!).

**Tip:** Before you fall in love with a name, check if it is free. There is nothing sadder than imagining your brand and seeing that someone else already uses it. Have options A, B, C and D, **be prepared** to have these options or look for a more regional internet domain like .mx, .co, or another.
