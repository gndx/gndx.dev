The battle of the Frontends, The dilemma arises when choosing between “Vue”, “Angular” or “React”. Which one is really better? Which one should I learn? Which is better paid? In this talk we present the pros and cons of the main frameworks and libraries for web development, we will focus on current labor market data as well as community support and everything there is to know about vue, angular and React.
