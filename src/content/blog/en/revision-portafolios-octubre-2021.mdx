## Reviewing portfolios for Subscribers

One of the most fun dynamics we have on my [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCw05fUBPwmpu-ehXFMqfdMw) is the review of my subscribers' portfolio, blog, and web pages, where we review in detail the proposals sent, recommend improvements, or talk about how well they have implemented their sites on the Internet.

We return to the dynamic this Friday, October 22, where we will review 10 projects and randomly we will also review the source code of a project in order to learn.

### How to Participate?

You must have your project deployed and open source and follow the following instructions.

_Send your Portfolio/Blog/Website_

1. Fork this repository. [portfolio-review](https://github.com/gndx/portfolio-review)
2. Add your URL to the list.
3. Send your Pull Request. Don't forget to fill out the required form.
4. See you on LIVE.

Friday, October 22, 2021 9:45 P.M. at [YouTube.com/oscarbarajas](https://youtu.be/EvCAJKMXOpE)
